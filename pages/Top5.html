Top 5 Most Beautiful stadiums in the World 2021:

News

(Updated 2021)

Football is definitely the most viewed sport in the world with events such as the world cup attracting well over 3 billion annual viewers.With such a demand for the sport it is no surprise football clubs around the globe have invested billions so as to accomodate their legions of fans.Here is our top 5 list:

A.)WEMBLEY,UK Wembley stadium is Arsenal's homeground.With an accommodation of 90,000 fans,the mega-stadium covers well over 11 acres.It has undergone several maintenance upkeeps since its first opening.To say the least Wembley has won the hearts of all gunnners around the world hence earning the top spot in our list.

B.)ALLIANZ ARENA,Munich. Allianz arena is Germany's best football stand as well as top 3 of Europe's best football stands.At night it can change its colours to the colours of the home team(Red and White).It is the homeground to Bundesliga's Bayern Munich and can accommodate well over 75,000 fans with an area covering almost 7.2 acres of land.

C.)OLD TRAFFORD,UK Almost every football fan knows Old Trafford as home to the Red Devils, and associated with great icons such as the late Sir Alex.It was first opened in 1910 and can fully accomodate well over 75,000 fans.Its area covers a massive 7.5 acres and is currently England's largest soccer stadium.

D.)CAMP NOU,SPAIN Camp Nou is Barcelona's homeground and has been its homeground since1957.El Classico derby is one of the most watched football competitions and a fair amount of the derbies have been hosted at Camp Nou.It spans over an area of 13.6 acres and can accomodate upto 99,000 Barca fans!

E.)SANTIAGO BERNABEU,MADRID Santiago Bernabeu is Spain's second largest soccer stadium.Located in Madrid, it is Real Madrid's homeground and was first opened in 1947.It spans an area of 9 acres and when ful can accomodate 81,000 fans.
