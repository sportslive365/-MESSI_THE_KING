Top 5 Most Expensive Football Transfers of all Time (2021)

Top 5

Soccer is an expensive affair and has always been an expensive affair.Its no surprise football transfers come at a hefty price.Since the end of the 19th century, football transfer deals have gone from costing couple hundreds of dollars to a whooping hundreds of milions of dollars.The most expensive sign in currently stands at 222 million Euros! Here is our top 5 list of the most expensive transfer deals of all time:

1.)NEYMAR DA SILVA SANTOS JNR. Born on November 1992,Neymar is regarded by many as one of the greates footballers of all time.Playing as a forward,he made his international debut aged 17 and was soon acquired by Barca as a forward alongside Luiz Suarez and Messi.It was his transfer from Barca to PSG that cost 222 milion Euros making him the most expensive player ever.

2.)MBAPPE Mbappe born 20 December 1998 is the French teenager who played for France during the 2018 World Cup in an outstanding performance that saw him become the second teenager to score in a world cup final after Pele.Its no surprise that his 180 milion Euro transfer to PSG was the most expensive domestic transfer with Mbappe becoming the most expensive teenager.

3.)PHILIPPE COUTINHO Coutinho born 1992 is a Brazilian Barca winger who was born in Rio.He signed for La Liga side Barcelona for an outstanding fee of 160 milion Euros and has since helped the team win 2 La Liga titles.

4.)JOAO FELIX Born 10 November 1999,Joao Felix is known for his speed and nice finish.Playing for Portugal as a forward ,he made his professional debut ate age 17 and played for Benfica helping them win the league title.Atletico signed him in 2019 for a 126 milion Euro fee making him the second most expensive teenager.

5.)ANTOINE GRIEZMANN Antoine Griezmann ,born 21 March 1991 is a French professional player who plays as a forward for both Barca and the French national team.His 2019 move from Atletico Madrid to La Liga giants Barca saw Barca cough a 120 milion Euro transfer fee making his transfer the fifth all time most expensive transfer.
