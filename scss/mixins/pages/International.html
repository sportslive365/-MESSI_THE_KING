Star of the Week: Renato Luz Sanches

News

Renato Lus Sanches,born Renato Junior Luz Sanches is a Portuguese player who plays as a midlefielder for both His national team,Portugal and for League 1's team Lille.Born on 18 August1997,Sanches career has been nothing shortof outstanding having made his full international debut in March 2016 ate age 17.Since then he has won several awards including being chosen for the UEFA Euro 2016 at age 18.

It was at Benfica that Sanches began his career in 2014 and helped Benfica win the Primeira Liga in the process being awarded Golden Boy and the Primeira Liga Breakthrough Player. Since then Bundesliga side Bayern Munich signed him for a record 35 million Euros(all time highest fee for any Portuguese player in the Primeira Liga).However during his first season he hardly played as a result of stiff competition and poor performance and was quickly loaned to Swansea City.He returned to Bayern in August 2019 and was signed by Lille for 25 million Euros becoming Lille's most expensive signing ever.With his help Lille won its first Ligue 1 title in ten years.Sanches has won 40 caps and scored 8 goals for Portugal,was the youngest player to win a Euro final,and youngest Portuguese to play in an international competition scoring one goal during the competition.To say the least Renato Lus Sanches is an outstanding player and definitely a promising youth to watch out for.
