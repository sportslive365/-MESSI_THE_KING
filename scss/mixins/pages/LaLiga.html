Who is Antonella Roccuzzo (Lionel Messi's wife)?

Trending

August, 2021

Compared to Christiano Ronaldo's self-confidence,Lionel Messi is quite a humble player and litle is known about his love life,wife and childhood sweetheart(since age5) Antonella Rocuzzo.Born in February 26 1988(only months after Messi), Roccuzzo much like Messi has lived her childhood life in a small Argentine town by the name of Rosario.She and Messi met through her cousin and Messi's best friend Lucas Scaglia, a professional football player at the age of five.

However, they soon separated with Messi moving to Barcelona, Spain at the age of 13 to play for the Barcelona youth team. Initially studying for dentistry at university level,Roccuzzo switched to the modelling career and has since modelled for major global brands.On Instagram she boasts of having well over 15 million followers and fans.Even after separating with Messi, they both kept in touch and would later have a re-union with Messi travelling to Rosario to comfort her after she lost a friend.Since 2008 they had reportedly been in a relationship but it was in 2009 that their relationship was first made public.They both have three sons: Thiago(born 2012),Mateo( born 2015) and Ciro(born 2018).
