Good news for Mtito-a-Ndei sex workers as DP Ruto pledges Sh1m

News

Brian Mwaura | October, 2021

Deputy President William Ruto has promised to give Mtito Andei commercial sex workers Sh1 million.

Speakingat a rally in Makueni County on Monday, DP Ruto told the call girls to group themselves into SACCOs so that they can receive the cash. Ruto asked the sex workers to ensure they have started decent business once they receive the money.He said the United Democratic Alliance (UDA) is a strong national political party and will bring every Kenyan economic good, unite and bring a meaningful changes in Kenya.
